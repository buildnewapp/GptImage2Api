# About SdanceAI

SdanceAI is an independent AI video platform and API service. We provide a custom interface and workflow layer on top of third-party models (such as Seedance 1.5, Veo, and Sora) so users can create high-quality AI videos with native audio synchronization.

**Compliance Notice**: SdanceAI is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by model providers. Model names are used only to describe compatibility.

## Our Story

This is a story about passion and creation.

Paidax, the founder of SdanceAI, is a dedicated developer and a loving father. During a rare holiday break, instead of simply resting, he decided to use this time to turn his vision for AI video generation technology into reality.

As a "stay-at-home dad" during his break, Paidax understands the value of time and the importance of creativity. He built SdanceAI not only to showcase the capabilities of modern AI video models (including Seedance 1.5), but also to prove that even with a busy life, passion and action can lead to the creation of amazing products, even during fragmented spare time.

This project was born out of a love for technology and hope for the future. We hope SdanceAI becomes the bridge connecting you to the world of AI video creation.

## Our Mission

Our mission is simple: **To make AI video creation accessible to everyone.**

Whether you are a user exploring AI video creation through SdanceAI or a developer wanting to build your own AI application via API, SdanceAI is here to provide reliable support.

## Our Services

- **Multi-Model Online Experience**: Experience high-quality AI video generation directly on the web, including supported models such as Seedance 1.5.
- **Native Audio Sync**: Enjoy an immersive experience where visuals and sound are naturally synchronized.
- **Developer API**: We provide stable and efficient API interfaces for developers to quickly integrate AI video features.
- **Subscription Management**: Users can directly manage and cancel subscriptions from Dashboard > Subscription via billing portal.
