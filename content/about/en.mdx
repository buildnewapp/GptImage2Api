# About SdanceAI

SdanceAI is the premier online platform for experiencing Seedance 2.0 and a provider of robust API services. We are dedicated to enabling users to create high-quality AI videos with native audio synchronization. Furthermore, we provide stable API access for developers to seamlessly integrate AI video capabilities into their applications.

## Our Story

This is a story about passion and creation.

Paidax, the founder of SdanceAI, is a dedicated developer and a loving father. During a rare holiday break, instead of simply resting, he decided to use this time to turn his vision for AI video generation technology into reality.

As a "stay-at-home dad" during his break, Paidax understands the value of time and the importance of creativity. He built SdanceAI not only to showcase the powerful capabilities of Seedance 2.0 but also to prove that even with a busy life, passion and action can lead to the creation of amazing products, even during fragmented spare time.

This project was born out of a love for technology and hope for the future. We hope SdanceAI becomes the bridge connecting you to the world of AI video creation.

## Our Mission

Our mission is simple: **To make AI video creation accessible to everyone.**

Whether you are a user looking to experience the stunning effects of Seedance 2.0 or a developer wanting to build your own AI application via API, SdanceAI is here to provide the most solid support.

- **Seedance 2.0 Online Experience**: Experience high-quality AI video generation directly on the web without complex configurations.

- **Developer API**: We provide stable and efficient API interfaces for developers to quickly integrate AI video features.
